The Orchid Flower, Its Meanings and Symbolism

Orchids are the largest family of blooming flowers with over 25,000 species and over 100,000 varieties. They are often grown as houseplant, or added to floral displays. But,not all orchids are tropical beauties. Wild orchids grow worldwide and can be found on every continent except Antarctica.These flowers have earned the reputation as difficult to grow, probably due to their need for filtered light and hight relative humidity. Many varieties, such as the moth orchid (Phalaenopsis) are surprisingly easy to grow as a houseplant.
